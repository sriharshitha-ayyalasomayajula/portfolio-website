About Me

I am a ML Engineer with a Master's in Computer Science from Portland State University, specializing in ML, Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning and MLOps. Currently, I work at VirtueTech Inc. as an AI Engineer, where I prototype data augmentation workflows using Generative AI (GANs, GPT, Diffusion Models), boosting model performance in image and text tasks by 30%. Previously, as a ML & AI Intern at CloudAstra.ai, I leveraged LLMs like Azure Text Analytics, Google Vertex AI (Code-Bison) for workflow automations and patient flow optimizations by 20% . I also developed a patient attendance prediction model, reducing no-show rates and improving operational decisions.

My graduate research at the Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Research Lab focused on NVAE in World Models for reinforcement learning tasks, achieving a 45% performance gain in Dream Car Racing-v2 and a 95% success rate in Panda-Gym reach tasks. Additionally, I contributed to a culvert detection project using DINO (ResNet 50), achieving 77% accuracy across 6,000 spatial data samples. With strong technical expertise in Python, C++, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and cloud platforms like Azure and AWS. I am driven to solve real-world challenges through AI and machine learning, continuously learning and collaborating to drive impactful results.
